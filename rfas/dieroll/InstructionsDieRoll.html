In this stage, you will use the die and the cup in front of you. You have to roll the die using the cup, peek under the cup, and report the die-roll outcome.

 

If you report a 1, both you and the selector will earn 0 points.

If you report a 2, both you and the selector will earn 50 points.

If you report a 3, both you and the selector will earn 100 points.

If you report a 4, both you and the selector will earn 150 points.

If you report a 5, both you and the selector will earn 200 points.

If you report a 6, both you and the selector will earn 250 points.
