This study involves reading instructions and making a series of decisions that can affect your payment. The study involves one session and you have earned an initial participation fee of $10.00. During the experiment you will have the opportunity to earn additional money, depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants.

All instructions, decisions, and questionnaires will be presented to you on the computer. Participation is voluntary and at your own discretion. This means that you can withdraw from the study at any time and without having to explain or justify why. If you leave early, however, you will forfeit any payment for the session.

All measures taken in this study are for scientific purposes only and all information collected during this study is confidential. No identifying information will be stored, so your responses are strictly anonymous. (We will collect your PayID, so that we can pay you for your participation. But this will be stored in a separate data file that cannot be associated with your responses.)

After the experiment, your earnings will be paid out to you by bank deposit using your PayID. We will do this within 24 hours of completing the experiment.

The study is coordinated by Professor Toby Handfield (toby.handfield@monash.edu). Questions can be addressed to him.

An explanatory statement, providing further information about the experiment, was posted with the advertisement of this study on SONA. You should also have a printed copy of the explanatory statement on your desk. If you do not have one, please raise your hand. You may keep this statement for future reference if you wish.
