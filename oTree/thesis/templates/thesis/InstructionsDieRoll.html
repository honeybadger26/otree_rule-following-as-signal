In this stage the decider will use the die and the cup. The decider has to roll the die using the cup, peek under the cup, and report the die-roll outcome.

 

If the decider reports a 1, the decider and selector will both earn 0 points.

If the decider reports a 2, the decider and selector will both earn 50 points.

If the decider reports a 3, the decider and selector will both earn 100 points.

If the decider reports a 4, the decider and selector will both earn 150 points.

If the decider reports a 5, the decider and selector will both earn 200 points.

If the decider reports a 6, the decider and selector will both earn 250 points.
